about us

about us

Australian International Institute of Technology Pty Ltd ATF AIIT Unit Trust trading as Australian International Institute of Technology is a CRICOS Registered Training Organisation based in Melbourne, Australia.

Australian International Institute of Technology is accredited and endorsed by the Department of Education and the National Regulator, Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA), and provides nationally recognised training, qualifications, and accredited courses in the following disciplines:

  • General English (Beginner to Advanced Level)
  • Leadership and Management
  • Commercial Cookery
  • Hospitality Management

We aim to provide our students a supportive and caring learning environment. Our qualified and accredited academic staff and our administrative support team aim is your success and we will do everything we can to support and help you to achieve your study goals.

Our mission and agenda is simple; to empower individuals for the world of tomorrow.
